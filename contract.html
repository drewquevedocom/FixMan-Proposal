1. Scope of Services

Provider agrees to deliver the following digital marketing services to Client:

  • Professional Website — Custom-built, mobile-first website for FixMan Appliance Repair (design, development, hosting, and ongoing maintenance)
  • AI Phone Receptionist — Voice AI agent that answers calls 24/7, books appointments, and handles FAQs
  • SMS Review & Follow-Up Agent — Automated review requests after service calls, appointment reminders, and customer follow-ups
  • Local SEO & Content — Google Business Profile optimization, blog content creation, local keyword targeting, and monthly updates
  • Email & CRM — Customer communication management using existing email infrastructure
  • Monthly Performance Reports — Summary of leads generated, rankings, reviews gained, and key metrics

2. Pricing & Payment

Monthly Retainer: $200/month

For the first 3 months, all AI agent usage costs (voice, SMS) are included in the $200 monthly fee — zero additional charges.

After Month 3: AI agent usage costs (voice minutes, SMS messages) are passed through to Client at cost plus a 20% service fee. Typical additional cost: ~$16–$40/month depending on call and SMS volume. Client will be notified of any changes before they take effect.

Website Build: The initial website design and development is provided at no charge as a portfolio investment by Provider.

Payment is due on the 1st of each month. First payment is collected upon signing this agreement.

3. Pay As You Grow

This is a pay-as-you-grow partnership designed for small business. As Client's business grows and more advanced marketing capabilities are needed, services may be expanded to include additional AI agents (Lead Generation, Sales Outreach, Website Chatbot, Customer Success, Analytics Dashboard) — up to the full 7-Agent AI Swarm package.

Each month, sufficient growth is noted and pricing will be adjusted accordingly. Any pricing adjustments will be communicated in advance and must be fair for both parties. Client is never locked into a higher tier — if volume decreases, pricing adjusts back down.

4. Term & Cancellation

Month-to-month. No long-term contract. Cancel anytime.

Either party may terminate this agreement with 14 days written notice (email is sufficient). Upon cancellation:

  • Client retains ownership of all content created for their business (blog posts, images, copy)
  • Website domain and hosting credentials will be transferred to Client within 7 business days
  • Any pre-paid amounts for unused service days will be prorated and refunded
  • AI agent phone numbers and accounts can be transferred to Client upon request

5. Ownership & IP

Client owns all content created specifically for their business, including website copy, blog posts, and images. Provider retains the right to showcase the work in their portfolio with Client's permission. The underlying code and templates used remain the intellectual property of Provider.

6. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep confidential any proprietary business information, customer data, financial details, and trade secrets shared during the course of this partnership. This obligation survives termination of the agreement.

7. Limitation of Liability

Provider's total liability under this agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by Client in the preceding 3 months. Provider is not liable for any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages including lost profits or business interruption.
